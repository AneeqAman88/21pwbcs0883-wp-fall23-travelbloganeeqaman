Lake Saif-ul-Mulook

This high-altitude lake is famous for its crystal-clear blue waters and is surrounded by snow-capped peaks. It's a popular trekking destination and offers breathtaking views.

Lalazar

Lalazar is a lush green plateau with stunning landscapes. The meadows are surrounded by forests and are a great spot for picnics and short hikes.

Babusar Pass

This high mountain pass offers spectacular views of the surrounding peaks and valleys. It's typically covered in snow and is accessible in the summer months.

Jalkhad

A serene spot along the Kunhar River, Jalkhad is perfect for trout fishing and picnics. The riverbanks are adorned with lush greenery.

Shogran

Shogran is a hill station known for its apple orchards and lush landscapes. You can take a jeep ride to Siri and Paye Meadows from here.
